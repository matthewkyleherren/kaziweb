Sezane

View project

Created in 2013 by Morgane Sézalory, Sézane was born from a desire to offer women the finest quality pieces with a Parisian touch. Numbered closely collaborates with them since 2018 on all their digital supports. We worked on a multi-device e-commerce, the IOS app, "Demain" the association, on the CSR program and on their event website. Each year, the brand reinvents itself and we are very proud to support them in their constant evolution!
